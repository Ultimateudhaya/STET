STET Eligibility Criteria 2021


Aspirants applying for Sikkim Teacher Eligibility Test (STET) 2021 have to check for the STET Eligibility Criteria 2021 beforehand to avoid dismissals in further stages.

Primary Level Teachers Eligibility

The candidates must have completed senior secondary or any of its equivalent education with minimum 50% marks and candidates who have passed or appearing in 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education/2-year Diploma in Education (Special Education)/ 4 years Bachelor of Elementary Education (B.El.Ed.).

Elementary Level Teachers Eligibility

The candidate should be a Graduate with at least 50% marks and qualified or appearing in the 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education or four years Bachelor in Elementary Education (B.El.Ed.)/ Bachelor in Education (B.Ed.)/ 4-year B.A.Ed./B.Sc.Ed./1-year B.Ed. (Special Education) shall be eligible for elementary level teachers.

Age limit for Sikkim Teacher Eligibility Test (STET) 2021 is 18 to 35 years. There will be age relaxation for the candidates who fall under the reserved category. Candidates must be a citizen of India/ Nepal/ Tibet/ Bhutan. The age limit and nationality requirements stated are eligibility criteria applicable to candidates for both level exams.


STET Selection Process 2021


The Sikkim Teacher Eligibility Test 2021 basically has 2 rounds in the selection process. They are:

Written Examination - Level I/Paper-I/ Primary Level (Class 1 to 5) and Level II/ Paper-II/Upper Primary level (Class 6 to 8). Personal round of Interview

Only the candidates who clear the written examination shall qualify for the Interview round. Candidates who clear the personal interview round shall be recruited.
